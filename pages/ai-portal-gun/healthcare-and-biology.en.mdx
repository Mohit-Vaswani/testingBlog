# AI in Healthcare & Biology: Empowering the Future of Medicine

AI in medicine involves the application of artificial intelligence techniques to enhance various aspects of healthcare. It encompasses tasks like medical image analysis, disease prediction, personalized treatment recommendations, drug discovery, and more. AI's capacity to analyze vast datasets and recognize patterns holds great promise for improving diagnostics, treatment, and patient outcomes, ultimately advancing the field of medicine.

### LLMs in Healthcare

- [Med-PaLM 2](https://sites.research.google/med-palm/) by Google Research, is an AI model tailored for the medical domain, available for limited testing by select Google Cloud customers. It excels in USMLE-style questions, reaching 'expert' performance on the MedQA dataset, and handling diverse biomedical data types. Healthcare organizations like HCA Healthcare, Mayo Clinic, and Meditech have tested it to augment healthcare workflows.

- [ClinicalBERT](https://huggingface.co/medicalai/ClinicalBERT) trained on a vast dataset containing 1.2B words related to various diseases. Fine-tuning utilized <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electronic_health_record">EHRs</a> from 3 million patient records. ClinicalBERT, initiated from <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://huggingface.co/docs/transformers/model_doc/bert">BERT</a>, employs a masked language model training approach. Text segments with randomly replaced tokens <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://huggingface.co/tasks/fill-mask">[MASK]</a> challenge the model to predict the original tokens in a contextual context.

- [Gatortron: The Biggest Clinical Language Model](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/on-demand/session/gtcspring21-s32030/), a clinical language model by the University of Florida & NVIDIA, uses over 90-B words from clinical notes, PubMed, and Wikipedia. With 8.9-B parameters, it excels in clinical NLP tasks, showing improved performance in concept extraction, relation extraction, textual similarity, inference, and medical question answering. As the largest transformer in clinical domains, GatorTron enhances healthcare by understanding and utilizing patient information from <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electronic_health_record">EHRs</a>.

### Advancements

- [AlphaFold](https://www.deepmind.com/research/highlighted-research/alphafold) by DeepMind, revolutionizes protein structure prediction, using deep learning to decipher the 3D shapes of proteins with remarkable accuracy. Its capabilities hold immense promise for understanding diseases and drug development, making it a game-changer in the field of bioinformatics and molecular biology. <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://github.com/google-deepmind/alphafold">(code)</a> 

- [Evolutionary Scale Modeling](https://github.com/facebookresearch/esm): ESM uses AI to predict protein structures through evolutionary data embeddings. Trained on millions of protein sequences, <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://huggingface.co/docs/transformers/model_doc/esm">ESM</a> aids in COVID-19 evolution prediction and disease genetic discovery. Key features include direct structure prediction, atomic-level precision, and leading performance with <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://huggingface.co/docs/transformers/model_doc/esm">ESM-2</a>. <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://ai.meta.com/blog/protein-folding-esmfold-metagenomics/">(blog)</a>

- [RoseTTAFold](https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.abj8754): By the Baker lab at the University of Washington, utilizes deep learning to swiftly predict protein structures in just ten minutes on a gaming computer. This "three-track" neural network considers protein sequences, amino acid interactions, and potential 3D structures simultaneously. Applied globally, it has solved long-standing biological problems, including understanding poorly known proteins and modeling complex biological assemblies. The latest version, <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://github.com/RosettaCommons/RFdiffusion">RF Diffusion</a>, enhances functional protein prediction with a diffusion model.

- [OmegaFold](https://github.com/HeliXonProtein/OmegaFold): A high-res <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/De_novo_protein_structure_prediction#:~:text=In%20computational%20biology%2C%20de%20novo,decades%20while%20still%20remaining%20unsolved.">De novo protein structure</a> prediction tool by HeliXonProtein, utilizes deep learning and a LLM for accurate protein structure predictions from primary sequences. Open-source and versatile, it aids in applications like COVID-19 evolution prediction and identifying genetic disease causes. <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2022.07.21.500999v1">(paper)</a> 

- [ESMFold](https://huggingface.co/facebook/esmfold_v1): Meta AI's protein prediction tool, employs <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://huggingface.co/docs/transformers/model_doc/esm">Evolutionary Scale Modeling (ESM)</a> and a trained LLM to predict a protein's 3D structure from its amino acid sequence. With the <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://huggingface.co/docs/transformers/model_doc/esm">ESM-2</a> model, it swiftly predicts one million biological sequences in under a day. Integrated into <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://www.cgl.ucsf.edu/chimerax/">ChimeraX</a>, ESMFold is a powerful tool for protein structure forecasting. <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://ai.meta.com/blog/protein-folding-esmfold-metagenomics/">(blog)</a>

- [AlphaMissense](https://deepmind.google/discover/blog/a-catalogue-of-genetic-mutations-to-help-pinpoint-the-cause-of-diseases/): DeepMind's AlphaMissense AI tool accurately categorizes 71 million human <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://www.genome.gov/genetics-glossary/Missense-Mutation">DNA 'missense' mutations</a> as likely pathogenic or benign with 89% accuracy. This accelerates molecular biology research, aids rare disorder diagnosis, and contributes to genetic disease treatment development by predicting the impact of genetic mutations. <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://www.science.org/stoken/author-tokens/ST-1429/full">(paper in Science)</a> <a class="link" target="_blank" href="https://github.com/google-deepmind/alphamissense">(code)</a>

### Articles & Papers

- [AI in Healthcare](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC8285156/): Gain profound insights into how AI is revolutionizing the healthcare industry, from diagnosis to treatment, research, and patient care.

- [AI for healthcare](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/stories/healthcare-ai/) at Imperial College London plays a pivotal role in the integration of AI into the healthcare sector. Collaboration and data standardization are imperative for efficient and ethical AI deployment.
